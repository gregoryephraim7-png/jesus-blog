JESUs is love

Jesus doesn’t just show love, He is love in motion. His love is:

  • unconditional (John 3:16)
  • sacrificial (John 15:13)
  • eternal (Jeremiah 31:3)

Loving people is not occasional for Him—it’s constant. He healed out of love, rebuked out of love, died out of love, and still calls us by love.

Love as Lifestyle

If love were a hobby, Jesus practices it daily—without fatigue, without limit, without hesitation.
