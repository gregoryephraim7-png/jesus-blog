Jesus is the light

Light – His Natural Glow Darkness can’t comprehend Him, overpower Him, or cancel Him.

  • He exposes evil (John 1:5)
  • He guides the lost (Psalm 119:105)
  • He illuminates hearts (2 Corinthians 4:6)

Light You Can’t Dim

Where Jesus enters, confusion exits. Shadows flee. Purpose awakens.
